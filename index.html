🍽 Your Daily Meal Plan

Simple, practical, and delicious options for your 9-5 schedule.

7:00 AM

🌅 Morning Start

  • Warm water with lemon
  • 4 soaked almonds + 1 walnut
8:30 AM

🥣 Breakfast

  • Option 1: Vegetable oats / poha / upma + 1 boiled egg
  • Option 2: 2 multigrain roti + sabzi + curd
  • 1 cup green tea / herbal tea
11:00 AM

🍎 Mid-morning Snack

  • 1 fruit (papaya / apple / orange)
  • Handful roasted seeds (pumpkin / sunflower)
1:30 PM

🍛 Lunch

  • 2 multigrain rotis / 1 bowl brown rice
  • Dal / chana / rajma
  • Seasonal sabzi (broccoli, spinach, etc.)
  • Fresh salad (cucumber, carrot, tomato)
4:30 PM

☕ Evening Snack

  • Roasted makhana / chana
  • 1 cup green tea
8:00 PM

🍲 Dinner

  • Option 1: Soup + grilled veggies + egg / grilled chicken
  • Option 2: 2 rotis + sabzi + dal
  • Option 3: Grilled salmon + sautéed veggies (1-2x/week)
